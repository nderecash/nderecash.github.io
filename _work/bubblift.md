## The game

Bubblift is a puzzle game about ocean pollution: clean bubbles rise naturally,
polluted ones need help, and the deeper you go the more hostile the water
chemistry. Simple mechanic, legible theme, shipped complete in 48 hours.

## What I did

In a three-person team (design, art, code), I designed the **game
architecture, level flows and the core balancing mechanic**, working in the
Godot engine. No purchased assets, no template — a full playable game from
zero in one weekend.

## What it proves

I can scope honestly, design a mechanic that carries a message, and ship
under extreme time pressure with people I'd just met.

I wrote up the full 48 hours — the team composition call, the dangerous first
six hours, what broke — in
[48 Hours to Build a Game: What Global Game Jam Taught Me]({{ '/writing/2025/04/03/building-bubblift-at-global-game-jam/' | relative_url }}).
