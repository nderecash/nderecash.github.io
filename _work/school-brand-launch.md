## The problem

Mary Queen of Peace Catholic Institute was opening four new schools at once.
No brand, no websites, no channels, no enrolment pipeline — and a fixed,
immovable launch date.

## What I did

As Marketing Manager (appointed at 24, leading a team of five across four
business units), I built the schools' entire digital existence from scratch:

- **Brand:** identity and brand guides for each school, coherent as a family.
- **Web and email:** four websites and the email systems behind them.
- **Social:** channel strategy, content production, and the applicant-facing
  journey from first impression to enrolment.
- **Launch:** organised the grand opening — **~1,500 attendees**, including the
  national Education Minister and international funders.

## Results

Four schools went from non-existent to fully branded, discoverable and
enrolling — websites, channels, email and campaigns live — by launch day.

## What it proves

I build things from zero under a hard deadline: brand systems, digital
infrastructure, and the event that announces them — while managing a team.
